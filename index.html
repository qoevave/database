360° Audiovisual Content

Up to 4th-order Ambisonics audio along with 8k resolution 360 degree video, for perceptual, cognitive, and behavioral research.

Available as audio only (.wav), video only (.mkv), or muxed 1st-order Ambisonics and video (.mp4), using ACN channel ordering and SN3D normalization (AmbiX format).

Ecological Validity

Real-life-like recordings taken at multiple locations representing daily life scenarios, aiming for content more ecologically valid in experimental environments.

  • Twelve different available scenes with a total of 26 different recordings.
  • Varying audio source properties, behaviors, and acoustic environments present accross the scenes.
  • Durations are on average 60 seconds per recordings.
  • Spatial and temporal video information available for all recordings.

Interactive CGI Scenes

Coming Soon: Three high-quality audiovisual 6-DoF CGI scenes made for audio research and development: The Mansion, The Cave, and The Cinema.

  • Gamification of audio-relevant user tasks for cognitive and behavioral analysis.
  • Plug-and-plug audio with Meta XR Audio SDK.
  • Additional acoustic geometric provided for more complex acoustic auralization with external tools.

3-DoF Saliency Data

Saliency data from 360° video presentation with no audio, mono audio, and 4th-order Ambisonics spatial audio.

Available as segmented (10 second) or culminated heat maps from 3-DoF head rotation data, along with scene description from free-verbalization task.
