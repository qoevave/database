A wooded footpath recorded near Goldbach, Nürnberg. The natural environment provides a low-distraction baseline with primarily ambient foliage and wind sounds. Versions vary the audio foreground content from pure natural ambience through to localizable and non-localizable music sources, enabling controlled study of music source salience and localizability.
