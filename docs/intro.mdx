# Available QoEVAVE Datasets

import Tabs from '@theme/Tabs';
import TabItem from '@theme/TabItem';

## [360 Scene Database](./Scenes)

- 12 different scene recordings (total of 26 sequences) average duration of 60s.
- Maximum video resolution is 8k.
- Audio available up to 4th-order Ambisonics in AmbiX format (ACN channel ordering & SN3D normalization).
- Downloads available for audio only (.wav), video only (.mkv), muxed audio and video (.mp4). 
- Spatial and temporal video information available for all sequences.
- Previews available on YouTube (with 1st-Order Ambisonics audio)

## [CGI Scene Database](./CGI)

- Three scenes for 6 degrees-of-freedom VR made with computer generated imagery.
- High-quality visual rendering made with Unity high-definition rendering pipeline (HDRP).
- Plug-and-play audio implementation with Meta XR Audio SDK.
- Comes with bespoke audio geometry that can be used in more high-fidelity audio rendering.
- Available as individual unity packages, or as complete Unity project.

## [Saliency Database](./Saliency)

- Head-tracking data captured at 50 Hz 
- Scene description captured after each video sequence through verbalization task.
- Cybersickness scores available, assessed through the simulator sickness questionnaire.
- All saliency data available for viewing 360 videos over head mounted display with three audio scenarios: no audio, mono audio, 4th-order Ambisoncis audio. 

## How can I download?

Download links for each database are provided on the [360](./Scenes)  and [CGI](./CGI) Scenes, and [Saliency](./Saliency) index pages. Alternatively, you can view each scene page for the respective databases and download scene specific data.
