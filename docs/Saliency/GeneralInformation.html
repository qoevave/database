General information about the Saliency-data-set

Data Description​

The download of the saliency data is spil up into two parts. On this page the general information about the Subjects (age, gender, prior experience with HMDs) and the ratings of the Simulator Sickness Questionnaire can be downloaded:

  • Subject Information and Simulator Sickness Questionnaire File: Subject_and_SSQ_Data.csv

On the specific sub-pages for each video, more information like the Head-Rotation Data or the Scene Verbalization Data can be downloaded.

If you want to download the data of all the scenes together at once, please you the following link:

  • All data combined: All_combined.zip
ℹ️ Naming Conventions

4OA Audio: SceneName+Version > A (audio) > AmbisonicsFormat+Order > Bit-depth

8K Video: SceneName+Version > V (video) > Resolution > Frames-per-second > Duration

YouTube: SceneName+Version > AV (audiovideo) > Resolution > Frames-per-second > Duration > AmbisonicsFormat+Order > Bit-depth
