## Overview 

The QoEVAVE Saliency Database is based on subjects **head-tracking** of free-form exploration of 14 360 degree videos presented over a head-mounted-display with three audio scenarios; *no audio, mono audio, and 4th-order head-tracked Ambisonics audio*. In addition to the head tracking data for the three audio scenarios, **scene descriptions** and **cybersickness scores** are collected through a post-viewing verbalization task and simulator sickness questionnaire.
