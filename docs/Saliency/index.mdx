## Overview 

The QoEVAVE Saliency Dataset captures head-tracking data from subjects freely exploring 14 360° videos presented over a head-mounted display. Each video was viewed under three audio conditions: no audio, mono audio, and 4th-order head-tracked Ambisonics. In addition to the head rotation data, participants completed a post-viewing verbalization task for scene description and the Simulator Sickness Questionnaire (SSQ) for cybersickness assessment.
